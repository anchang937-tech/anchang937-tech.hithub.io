我们关注的问题

Issues We Focus On

代际之间的数字鸿沟和情感隔阂是当代社会面临的重要挑战，让我们一起了解这些问题的具体表现。

The digital divide and emotional barriers between generations are important challenges facing contemporary society. Let's understand the specific manifestations of these issues together.

  • 数字鸿沟：对扫码支付、线上挂号等无所适从，产生被抛弃感和社交孤立

Advantages and Resources

深远意义

Profound Significance

代际桥梁项目不仅解决了表面的数字鸿沟问题，更在深层次上促进了代际融合与社会和谐。

The Intergenerational Bridge project not only addresses the superficial digital divide but also promotes intergenerational integration and social harmony at a deeper level.

对老年人的提升

心理福祉

Psychological Well-being

减轻孤独感，提升"被需要"的价值感与尊严，增强社会参与感和生活满意度。

Reducing loneliness, enhancing the sense of being needed, dignity, social participation, and life satisfaction.

功能福祉

Functional Well-being

增强独立生活能力，保持认知活跃度，提高数字素养，更好地适应现代社会。

Enhancing independent living abilities, maintaining cognitive activity, improving digital literacy, and better adapting to modern society.

对青年人的提升

心智成长

Mental Growth

获得情感支持与人生智慧，缓解知识焦虑，培养同理心和耐心，形成更全面的人生观。

Gaining emotional support and life wisdom, alleviating knowledge anxiety, cultivating empathy and patience, and forming a more comprehensive outlook on life.

能力锻炼

Ability Development

提升沟通、共情能力，深化社会理解，培养责任感，获得宝贵的社会实践经验。

Enhancing communication and empathy abilities, deepening social understanding, cultivating a sense of responsibility, and gaining valuable social practice experience.
